    .L'amage

    .L'amage (length 190 m): L'mage yacht based on a drawing by designer H. Bekradi from HBD Studios at Monaco Yacht Show in September 2016. As expected, the design of L'amage is 9.7 m longer than the world's longest superyacht Azzam (180.1 m). 2 diesel engines combined with a pair of gas turbines give L'amage a total capacity of 95,000 horsepower and a maximum speed of 32 knots. In addition to a private apartment for the owner and area for 70 members, L'amage is also developing 14 private rooms for a total of 28 guests.
