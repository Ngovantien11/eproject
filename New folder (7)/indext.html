    Specter

    Specter (30.33 m long): The smallest superyacht in the list of top Specters in terms of speed. Owning 3 1,900 horsepower diesel engines, AB100 Specter from Fila Group's AB Yachts has a speed of 84 km / h. Each engine is coupled with a water jet thruster for powerful, quiet operation. The stern area with an open design gives visitors the closest feeling to the sea.
